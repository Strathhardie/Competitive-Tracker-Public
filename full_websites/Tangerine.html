Savings Account, Chequing Account, and GIC interest rates expressed on this website are annual interest rates and are current as of today's date. Interest rates are subject to change without notice. Fair Fees What do we mean?
Canadians are fed up with high banking fees, and ever since we first opened our doors, we have shown our Clients that we feel the same way. Fees should be fair, which means they are disclosed and agreed to in advance, and the amount makes sense relative to the benefit received.
